About Us

The Women's Foundation is a non-profit organisation
dedicated to improving the lives of women and girls in Rwanda
We aspire to conduct groundbreaking research, to run
innovative and impactful community programmes and we
engage in education and advocacy in the pursuit of three
main goals:



Challenging gender stereotypes



Empowering women in poverty to achieve a better quality of
life for themselves and their families

Our Vision

Our vision is a Rwanda Woman Comunity in which women and girls have
the opportunity, support and resources to thrive and achieve
their true potentia

Our Mission

The Womens Foundation exists to promote the full and equal
participation of women and girls in all aspects of Rwanda
Howan Community, by engaging in research,programmes,
education and advocacy to drive measurable change

Our History

The Womens Foundation was established in 2022 after a group of like-minded
women identified the need for an organisation that would provide a voice for women
and girls in RHC Their primary objective was to tackle the challenges and inequities
that women and girls face across RHC society; a greater likelihood of being poorer and
lower paid, subject to domestic violence, subject to prejudice in many industries, and
less likely to achieve senior positions in the workplace or in politics

Our Principle

We value - in ourselves and in every organization we support
the core principles of equality, empowerment, diversity, collaboration,
and integrity.
